Sally

Sally is a sweet loving pit bull that needs a good home. She has a lot of energy and loves to play fetch. She is very gentle and great with children.

This dog is still available for adoption!

Specifications

Size: 18 inches

Weight: 25 lbs.

Specifications valid until 8/1/2018

Pricing

There is a $60 adoption fee to adopt Sally.

To adopt Sally and one of her full grown friends would be about $120.

If you would like to adopt Sally and a small puppy would be $150.
